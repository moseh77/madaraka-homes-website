About This Project

Greenfield Estate Phase 3 offers prime residential plots in the rapidly developing Juja area. With easy access to Thika Superhighway, schools, and shopping centers, this is an ideal location for families and investors alike.

All plots come with verified title deeds and ready infrastructure including access roads, water, and electricity connections. The estate is thoughtfully planned with designated green spaces, ensuring a healthy and vibrant community.

Juja has seen remarkable growth in recent years, driven by its proximity to Nairobi and the presence of major institutions like Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). This makes Greenfield Estate an excellent investment with strong appreciation potential.
